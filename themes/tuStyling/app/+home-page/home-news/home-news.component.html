Welcome to reposiTUm, the TU Wien institutional repository

With this service, the Library provides a platform for TU Wien researchers to publish their scientific works under Open Access conditions. The publications are archived, provided with a persistent identifier (URN) so that they can be reliably referenced, and made available worldwide online.

As of 2016, electronic versions of TU Wien theses are stored on this document server as well.

University staff of TU Wien are invited to deposit their research documents in reposiTUm. Most publishers permit authors to make their manuscripts open access in repositories. For details of journal embargo periods, check SHERPA/RoMEO.

For more information, please contact us at repositum@tuwien.ac.at
